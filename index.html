SOLUTIONS & SERVICES

Starting from a simple smart solution to a fully integrated home automation system, we customize to connect unlimited number of products to be controlled via a touch of a button at home or remotely. Acolux team will work closely with you to make your dream come true!

Energy Management

We track and optimize the energy consumption of buildings & homes to create an energy-efficient living/work space. The process starts from identifying the sources of energy consumption, collecting and analyzing continuous meter data and finally, proposing the required solution to minimize the overall energy consumption. This will result in decent savings on the energy bills with a guaranteed return on investment (ROI)

Solar Panels

Our team of experts are highly qualified to design the most suitable solar panel system for all project sectors. Our clients can enjoy the use of this solar power that is considered both a green and a renewable source of energy. Such solar panel systems can make it possible for someone to eliminate the electricity bills (off-grid) or minimize them (on-grid).

Lighting Fixtures

Electrical Preventive Maintenance

With Acolux Green Shield packages, our clients will have the peace of mind of a perfectly functional electrical network all the time. Inspection and maintenance of the electrical equipment will be conducted regularly as per the recommended schedule to avoid any possible failures in the future. Without regular inspection and maintenance, equipment failure can lead to catastrophic safety hazards, inefficiency, and inflated maintenance costs. The average failure rate of electrical equipment increases by %300 without an EPM program.
